UNLOCK UTOPIA - Driving online users from online to in-store through code redemption and incentivisation

To highlight the re-opening of their flagship London store, UNIQLO created 'This Way To Utopia', a multi-channel campaign living across TV, OOH, social, experiential, digital and in-store which celebrated London, diversity, and featured UNIQLO's lifewear products.

As part of the campaign, UNIQLO challenged Graphite to encourage customers to visit the store on launch day and to capture customer data - an important KPI for the brand in a bid to grow their direct audience base.

The solution:

Graphite created 'Unlock Utopia' using our Prize Media technology, a campaign of two parts:

1. Registration, where customers entered their details in order to receive a unique code which would be sent out via email.

2. Redemption - on the launch day customers would bring their code to the exhibition area on the roof of the store, where a host would check the code with Prize Media's redemption tool to reveal if they had won a prize.
