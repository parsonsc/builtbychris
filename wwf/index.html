In celebration of our Diamond Anniversary, the DofE is asking you to take part in our once-in-a-lifetime DofE-inspired Diamond Challenge and raise funds to help transform young lives near you.

There’s no limit to what you can do – big or small, if it’s a challenge for you it counts. Go it alone or form a team. And remember, current DofE participants can take on the Diamond Challenge as part of their DofE programme. Funds raised through the DofE Diamond Challenge will support our ambition to give more disadvantaged and vulnerable young people near you the opportunity to benefit from the DofE.
